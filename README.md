## Project: European PESTEL Audit & Market Research Validation (Sustainable Polymers)
**Files:** `Assessment 1.pdf` (Comprehensive Written Analysis) & `PESTEL ANALYSIS.pdf` (Executive Presentation Slides)

A rigorous macro-environmental (PESTEL) framework analysis evaluating the expansion viability of a French bio- and mineral-based plastic compound firm across the European Union. The project utilizes a unique methodology comparing preliminary AI-generated market baselines against verified secondary data streams to build a highly accurate corporate expansion blueprint.

### Core Business & Methodology Insights
* **The Strategic Validation Methodology:** Evaluated the operational delta between generic AI insights (ChatGPT) and quantified secondary research. Demonstrated that while AI effectively maps broad thematic indicators, precise cross-border compliance demands verified, source-complementary data.
* **Political & Economic Drivers:** Audited the impacts of the European Green Deal and regional funding mechanisms. Evaluated how evolving green subsidies and regional import/export structures change cost allocations for raw materials and finished bio-polymers.
* **Socio-Cultural Shifts:** Tracked modern consumer behavior transformations and Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) compliance initiatives, mapping out how eco-conscious consumer shifts directly influence enterprise B2B procurement strategies.
* **Technological & IP Protection Innovation:** Leveraged WIPO (World Intellectual Property Organization) and FAME directories to analyze a sharp rise in "Green Chemistry" patents, demonstrating the critical need for strategic multi-country patent filings to protect innovative sustainable polymers.
* **Environmental & Legal Regulation Mapping:** Cross-referenced strict regulatory mandates from the EU Commission, WWF, and Eco-Emballages (France). Evaluated the strategic and financial impacts of Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) taxes, circular economy plastic roadmaps, and outright bans on conventional single-use plastics.
